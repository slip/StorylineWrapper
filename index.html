    Welcome to StorylineWrapper

    This is a quick and easy-to-use utility to wrap Storyline courses with a custom wrapper. There are two editions: IT Branded and DaVita Branded.

    Use

    1. Open the edition you plan to use (IT Branded for courses created by IT Launch, Learning and Development, or DaVita branded for all other courses.)
    2. Choose the wrapper you need from the drop-down menu based on the presence of resources in your course.
    3. Click the 'Find Course' button.
    4. Navigate to the output folder of your course. This is the folder created by Storyline which contains the story.html file.
    5. Your course should now have a custom wrapper. Preview your course by opening story.html in your browser.
    6. Zip your course and send it to Andy to put on the LMS.
